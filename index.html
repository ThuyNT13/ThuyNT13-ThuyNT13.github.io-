Evolution

Some changes from the original table-layout idea as it's not so pretty to look at on mobile devices. It is a bit much to squeeze in that information onto a device that only spans your hand.

Progress

Able to figure out some button styles for links. Utilizing border-radius, background, and other little changes, made it stand out. Also, with the a:hover selector, gave buttons a pop when the cursor is over it. Still deciding on whether or not I like the box-shadow effect.

More...

The main issue is trying to get my objects to align just where I want it to. Getting the column formation of the social media links to turn into a row...not happening. Centering is also a big hassle as margin:auto doesn't always work. Resisting float as the display property has so much potential for futzing with. Plus with display, I can play with flexbox!

Need to explore more responsive design techniques. Finding optimal font-size for readibility on mobiles as well as a large computer monitor is one of a few aspects I need to explore.

Getting rid of the ugly underlines for links cause the links to disappear into the text. Present solution feels clutzy.

Haven't really explored what font styles, color or other design issues as I'm still troubleshooting layout. Probably will move the Version section to the side and social media icons inline with nav buttons.
